Meet Your New Favourite Brewery

Based in the heart of Partick, Third Date Beer brings unconventional craft beer to Glasgow one glass at a time. Our mission? Keep it quirky, keep it fresh, and keep your taste buds entertained.

  • 🍻 Award-winning microbrewery vibes
  • ❤️ Small batch, big personality
  • 🏆 Top Glasgow taproom experience

Taste Buds on the Third Date

Whether you’re a hop fanatic, malt lover, or just beer-curious, our ever-changing selection has something to surprise your senses. Try our signature “Swipe Right IPA” or the bold “Awkward Silence Stout.” New taproom releases drop every month—stop in, sip up, and take your taste buds on a date to remember!
