About Our Groove

Since, like, forever, Record Shop Glasgow has been the local nerve center for lovers of the vinyl sound and dad joke connoisseurs. We believe music should be fun, a bit weird, and always heartfelt.

  • No laser discs. Ever.
  • More genres than you can shake a turntable at.
  • Staff that can name that tune in 3 notes.
  • Weekly blog updates with the snarkiest music takes.

Blog: Riffs & Quips

Our blog is where we wax lyrical about lost gems, roast the mainstream, and deliver our unfiltered (and slightly irreverent) takes on what’s spinning in Glasgow and beyond.
