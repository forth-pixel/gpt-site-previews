About Third Date Records

Third Date Records is not your typical vinyl spot. Nestled in the back of Third Date Coffee, we blend caffeine with hard-to-find bangers, offering a hand-picked mix of used and new vinyl treasures in the heart of Partick, Glasgow.

  • Curated selection of pre-loved and new records
  • Edgy, irreverent Glasgow vibe
  • Expert recommendations (and occasional questionable jokes)
  • Drop-in for a dig and a brew
