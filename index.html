About Third Date Beer

We're a fiercely independent microbrewery in the heart of Partick, Glasgow. Our beers are small-batch, big-flavour and laced with attitude (and a dash of humour). You’ll find Third Date Beer pouring in shops, pubs, and—most importantly—our lively brewery taproom. We’ve got the craft beer Glasgow has been thirsting for.

  • ✔ Quirky, award-ish craft beers brewed onsite
  • ✔ Legendary banter & local events
  • ✔ Edgy taproom experience with Glasgow soul

Craft Beer with Character

Our ever-evolving line-up: from hop-heavy IPAs to citrusy sours and the toffee-nutty ales that’ll win over your gran. Each pint is crafted with a wink—some with a cosmic sense of humour.

Hopped Up IPA

An unapologetically hoppy IPA bursting with tropical fruits and as bold as your third date chat.

6.2% ABV

Glasgow Red Ale

Rich, toffee, and just a wee bit nutty. A local favourite for those who like their beer with a story (and a punchline).

5.0% ABV

Partick Citrus Sour

Bright, tart, and totally refreshing. Perfect for summer or when your Tinder date turns weird.

4.2% ABV
